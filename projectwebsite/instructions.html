Reach: How to Use

Simple and Easy! Step 1: Go to Reach to get started Step 2: Enter your leftover ingredients that you have in the fridge Step 3: Click "Find Recipes" The results returns a table of the recipe names, links, what additional ingredient that you may need, and what was not used! They are all sorted by the most ingredient used recipes, so the results try to combine as many ingredients together as possible! Note: ingredients do not include common supplies like salt and sugar.
